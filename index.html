About

I'm Aric, a digital storyteller, data journalist and inherent creator of copy and code. I'm currently a legislative reporter for the Associated Press covering the Indiana Legislature and the coming state elections


In my experience so far, I've had a chance to wear a few hats in digital communication doing web producing, copy editing, news editing, beat and graphic reporting and social media strategy.


I've spent most of the previous few years as a reporter but have also had gigs in governnment and startups.


I've also just finished my undergraduate degree from Ball State's multimedia journalism program. I spent four years in the program learning how to leverage digital tools to best tell a story.


I spend a lot of time reading about trends in web publishing, investigative journalism, UX design and front-end methodologies. I usually tweet a lot of the ideas and material I come across, so connect with me on Twitter and see which ideas I'm tossing around.
