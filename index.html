Aric Chokey

Journalist and web developer

About

Lately, I've been hacking content as a web intern at the Pulitzer Prize-winning Tampa Bay Times and fresh off of the editorial team of the award-winning Ball State Daily News. While there I've helped carry the news section through our newspaper's digital-first transition as news editor.


Intrinsically, I'm a creator whether that be of copy or code. During my gigs at newspapers, I've worn a few hats doing web producing, copy editing, news editing, beat and graphic reporting and social media management. I've also spent some time at a startup and the Indiana Senate's communications office producing promotional content and growth hacking.


I've also just finished my undergraduate degree from Ball State's multimedia journalism program. I spent four years in the program learning how to leverage digital tools to best tell a story.


I spend a lot of time reading about trends in web publishing, product management, UX design and front-end methodologies. I usually tweet a lot of the ideas I come across, so connect with me on Twitter and see which ideas I'm tossing around.
