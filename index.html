Hi, I'm Aric.

I'm an intern at the Pulitzer Prize-winning Tampa Bay Times and a fresh Ball State University journalism grad and editorial team member at the award-winning Ball State Daily News where I've helped carry the news section through our newspaper's digital-first transition.

Though my background is in print, my interests are mainly in data journalism, front-end development and using design thinking to create stories and projects that are useful to the public. I'm always working on learning a new computer language and methods to web scraping and data manipulation.

Lately, I've been playing around a lot with D3 for data visualization and Tableau.
